Tips to Avoid Fake Jobs

  • Be wary of jobs promising very high pay for little work or no experience.
  • Never pay application, training, or equipment fees up front.
  • Check official company domain emails (avoid free webmail for formal offers).
  • Verify company existence via LinkedIn, company site, and news mentions.
  • Look for vague role descriptions with urgent pressure to apply immediately.
  • Avoid sharing personal IDs or banking info early in the process.
